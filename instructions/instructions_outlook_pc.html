Instructions for Outlook (PC)
  1. Using your mouse, highlight your Signature Preview to the right and hit Ctrl-c to copy it to your clipboard.
  2. In Outlook, open a new message. On the Message tab, in the Include group, click Signature, and then click Signaturesâ€¦
  3. On the Edit signature dialogue box click New.
  4. In the body field, hit Ctrl-v to paste the content of your clipboard.
  5. In Choose default signature, select your new signature for New messages and Replies/forwards.
  6. Click OK to save. For further help see Microsoft Office Support.
