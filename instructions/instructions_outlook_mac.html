Instructions for Outlook (Mac)
  1. Using your mouse, highlight your Signature Preview to the right and hit Command-c to copy it to your clipboard.
  2. Open a new message. On the Message tab, in the Include group, click Signature, and then click Edit Signatures.
  3. On the Edit signature dialogue box, click + (New).
  4. In the right column (the preview), hit Command-v to paste the content of your clipboard.
  5. Close the dialogue box and your signature will be saved.
  6. For further help see Microsoft Office Support.
