Instructions for Android Gmail app
  1. Send yourself an email message from a computer or device that already has your email signature installed. It can be just a blank email with your signature.
  2. Open the email in the Gmail app.
  3. Press and hold down on the signature in order to bring up the selection tool. Move the anchors so you have the entire signature highlighted.
  4. Tap on Copy.
  5. While in the app, tap the menu button, then More, then Settings
  6. Tap the entry for the email address you'd like to attach (or edit) the signature of
  7. Tap the entry that says Signature and paste the HTML generated by the LMU email signature tool.
  8. Save
