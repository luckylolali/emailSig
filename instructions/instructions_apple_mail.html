Instructions for Apple Mail
  1. Using your mouse, highlight your Signature Preview to the right and hit Command-c to copy it to your clipboard.
  2. In Mail, choose Mail > Preferences, then click Signatures.
  3. In the left column, select the email account you want to use the signature for, then click + (New).
  4. In the middle column, type a name for the signature.
  5. The name appears in the Signature pop-up menu in the message header when you write a message.
  6. In the right column (the preview), paste the HTML generated by the LMU email signature tool.
  7. For further help see Apple Mail Support
